HAB Accomodation Allocation :

The HAB shall allot accommodation (on availability) to the Institute Project Staff and the delegates/ sponsored persons/trainees from Institutions/ all others who are authorized to stay in the hostels for specified periods of duration in the hostels. All corroespondence related to accommodation in hostels should be addressed to the Chairman HAB.
